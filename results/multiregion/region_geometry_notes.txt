Region Geometry Diagnostics
===========================

Country masks use Natural Earth 1:50m country polygons and CHIRPS grid-cell centers.
They are a first-pass rectangular-bbox audit, not a replacement for basin polygons.

  Valid CHIRPS cells retained: 6,622/6,816 (97.15%); outside country: 194 (2.85%).
  Forecast dataset pixels retained: 6,622/6,816 (97.15%).
  Interpretation: Country geometry removes a small but measurable cell fraction; masked sensitivity is useful.
  Caveat: Country mask is only a land/country sanity check; it is not a Central Valley basin polygon.
