Region Geometry Diagnostics
===========================

Country masks use Natural Earth 1:50m country polygons and CHIRPS grid-cell centers.
They are a first-pass rectangular-bbox audit, not a replacement for basin polygons.
Source URL: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nvkelso/natural-earth-vector/master/geojson/ne_50m_admin_0_countries.geojson

horn_of_africa - Horn of Africa bounding box
  Countries: Djibouti; Eritrea; Ethiopia; Kenya; Somalia
  Valid CHIRPS cells retained: 53,577/61,379 (87.29%); outside country: 7,802 (12.71%).
  Forecast dataset pixels retained: 0/0 (nan%).
  Interpretation: Country geometry removes enough valid cells that a masked rerun should precede final claims.
  Caveat: Country mask is a first-pass Horn land mask; it is not a hydrologic or livelihood-zone boundary.
