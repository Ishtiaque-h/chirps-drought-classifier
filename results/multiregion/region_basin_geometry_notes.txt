Basin Geometry Diagnostics
==========================

Masks use official basin/district polygons and CHIRPS grid-cell centers.
They are stricter scientific region definitions than rectangular bounding boxes.

  Mask: Southern Great Plains EPA South Central Semi-Arid Prairies
  Source: US EPA Level III ecoregions with state boundaries; selected features in NA_L2NAME=SOUTH CENTRAL SEMI-ARID PRAIRIES that intersect the configured Southern Great Plains bbox.
  Valid CHIRPS cells retained: 19,424/24,700 (78.64%); outside basin: 5,276 (21.36%).
  Forecast dataset pixels retained: 19,424/24,700 (78.64%).
  Interpretation: Basin geometry materially changes the sample; masked rerun should be treated as the cleaner checkpoint.
